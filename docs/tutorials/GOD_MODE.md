## 2. The Toolkit üõ†Ô∏è

### Environmental Control: Shaping the Crucible üåç

The world is your canvas. Use the terrain and social brushes to define the boundaries of life.

* **Terrain Brushes (`$`, `%`, `^`)**:
    * `$` **Oasis**: Paint fertile ground to encourage population booms.
    * `%` **Wall**: Erect impassable barriers to isolate lineages or protect favorites.
    * `^` **Barren**: Create deserts to test the resilience of your subjects.
* **Social Brushes (`!`, `@`, `#`)**:
    * `!` **Peace Zone**: Enforce non-aggression. Ideal for breeding herbivores.
    * `@` **War Zone**: Heighten aggression and combat bonuses. The ultimate proving ground.
    * `#` **Neutral**: Reset the social climate of a region.

### Genetic Intervention: The Spark of Life ‚ö°

Directly manipulate the code of life to bypass the slow crawl of natural selection.

* **`X` Key: Genetic Surge**: Trigger a massive mutation event in the current population. Use this to break genetic stagnation and force new traits to emerge.
* **Right Click: Food Injection**: Manually place resources to guide migration patterns or save a starving favorite from the brink of extinction.

## 3. Walkthrough: Breeding a Master Race üß¨

Follow these steps to accelerate the evolution of a superior lineage:

1. **Isolate**: Use the **Wall brush (`%`)** to trap a promising population in a small, controlled area.
2. **Pressure**: Apply environmental stress. Use the **Barren brush (`^`)** to limit food, or introduce a predator. Only the strongest should survive.
3. **Mutate**: Press **`X`** to trigger a **Genetic Surge**. Watch for favorable mutations‚Äîhigher speed, better sensing, or increased aggression.
4. **Cull**: If a mutation is unfavorable, let nature (or your brushes) take its course. If a "Master" emerges, use **`C`** to export their HexDNA or **`W`** to save the world state.

## 4. Walkthrough: The Lazarus Project üíÄ

Death is not the end in *Primordium*. The **Fossil Record** remembers all.

1. **Enter Archeology Mode**: Press **`Y`** to open the historical browser.
2. **Find the Legend**: Use `[` and `]` to navigate through past snapshots. Look for extinct lineages that once dominated the world.
3. **Resurrect**: Once you've found a legendary genotype, press **`G`** to inject that DNA back into the current simulation. Watch as an ancient ghost walks the earth once more.
